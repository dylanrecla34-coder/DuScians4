About Our School

Ramon Teves Pastor Memorial - Dumaguete Science High School (RTPM-DSHS) is a public science high school in Dumaguete City, Philippines. It serves as the Regional Science High School for the Negros Island Region and is recognized by the Department of Education.

Located at Maria Asuncion Village, Barangay Daro, Dumaguete City, Negros Oriental. Our iconic pink uniforms represent unity, grace, and the vibrant spirit of our science scholars!

Brief History

The school opened its doors in June 1988 with DECS approval, offering a science-enriched curriculum for the first and second year levels. It was established to provide quality science and mathematics education to intellectually gifted students of Dumaguete City and Negros Oriental.

Named after the late Congressman Ramon Teves Pastor, it has grown into the premier science high school in the region, producing top achievers in national and regional competitions.
